Simple project setup for creating world composite maps for the game Conan Exiles

Demo Video - https://youtu.be/bxuPzWLCFw4

Heightmap Generater used in demo video is TerreSculptor Standard Edition, it's free and can be used commercially at the date of this upload (30/6/2017)
TerreSculptor Download Links - http://www.demenzunmedia.com/home/support/


Notes - water is placeholder, it should be deeper in most cases and the water plane (mesh) should be subdivided (for culling) to increase performance.
